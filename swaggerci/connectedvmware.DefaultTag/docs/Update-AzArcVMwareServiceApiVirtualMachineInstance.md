## SYNOPSIS

 [-HardwareProfileNumCpUs <Int32>] [-NetworkProfileNetworkInterface <INetworkInterfaceUpdate[]>]
 [-StorageProfileDisk <IVirtualDiskUpdate[]>] [-DefaultProfile <PSObject>] [-AsJob] [-NoWait] [-Confirm]
 [-WhatIf] [<CommonParameters>]
